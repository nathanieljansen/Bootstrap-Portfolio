About Me

Hi I'm Nathaniel Jansen from Westminster CO. I am just now starting this exciting journey into web deveopement. While I've of done some(I mean a little) html and css in in the past, I am now diving in all the way.
For more than 10 years I worked in the automotive industry providing dealers with online marketing of their vehicles. We would provide phtographs and data entry, along with window stickers and Buyers Guides in order to help customers make an informed decision. While I grew through the ranks of the corporate ladder, I always had it in the back of my mind that I would like to achieve more, make something more and become something more.
